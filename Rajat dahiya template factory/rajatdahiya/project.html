Bahadurgarh city located in Jhajjar District in the state of Haryana, India. The city comprises 31 wards and is approximately 2 km from National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi and 31 km from Jhajjar city, the district Headquarter. It is one of the major cities of Haryana and is surrounded by the major NCR cities of Faridabad, Gurugram & Sonipat. Bahadurgarh is also known as the "Gateway of Haryana".
